I’m so glad you’re here! My name is Allison Wagner, I’m the blogger behind West Coast Aesthetic and an Art Director at Amazon. I’m a Seattle-based girl with a colorful soul. I’m a lover of 70’s music, sunshine and enneagram personality tests (I’m an ENFJ). In my spare time I enjoy traveling, listening to crime podcasts and creating magic with other creatives.

West Coast Aesthetic is the fashion, travel and lifestyle blog of Allison Wagner. This blog aims to capture the life of a twenty-something and the experiences and inspirations that ensue along the west coast and beyond.
